## 🖌 Overview  

Crypto Pro Professional Suite is a full-featured market intelligence and portfolio management platform for cryptocurrency investors who need accurate, real-time data presented in a way that makes their portfolio easy to understand and act on.

The watchlist system lets you monitor hundreds of assets across multiple categories — top coins, DeFi tokens, NFT projects, and custom groups — each with live price feeds and customizable alert triggers.

Exchange integration pulls your actual holdings from connected accounts, eliminating manual entry and ensuring your portfolio view is always accurate without constant updates.

Market overview dashboards show dominance, trending assets, top gainers and losers, and global market cap data in a layout that gives you a quick read on market conditions whenever you open the app.

Crypto Pro Professional Suite is the portfolio intelligence tool that serious cryptocurrency investors use to stay informed and in control of their holdings across every market condition.
